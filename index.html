There are many trees on our mother Earth

























They stand tall, vibrant with leaves







We utilize them for many things from paper to buildings.






We collect fruit from their branches.

We use their sap and leaves for sweetners and hair products.







Humanity benefits from many of the trees' properties.

But the most important benefit from trees is their ability to create the clean air we breath in, taking in the carbon dioxide and letting out oxygen.




















And yet...

















Humanity continues to let these trees burn.

Many have been taken down for the benefit of people's businesses. Others harm the trees without even realizing.

From 2013 to 2022, there were an average of 61,410 wildfires annually and an average of 7.2 million acres impacted annually.





























But there are ways to help.

We can recycle

Reduce your carbon footprint

Plant more trees

Educate and spread the word of the importance of trees

Without these trees, we wouldn't have the oxygen we breath.
